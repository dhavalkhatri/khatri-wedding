Frequently Asked Questions

How far is the VITS Kamats Resort? - https://www.vitshotels.com/riverside-resort-silvassa/

VITS Kamats Resort is located near Daman Ganga River and Swaminarayan Temple. The Resort is 3.8 kms away from Silvassa Bus Station, 22.2 kms away from Vapi Railway Station and Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport is the nearest Airport with approximate distance is 157 kms. Guest will arrange their own transportation to reach the resort.

Will transportation be provided from the Resort to the wedding venue?

Absolutely. We will have Buses leaving from The VITS Kamats Resort as part of Jaan Prasthan to reach at wedding ceremony Venue at Valsad on time. We will also have Buses to take you back to the Resort at the end of the night.

What is Pithi?

Pithi is the paste that made of chickpea flour, turmeric, rose water and other ingredients. Since ancient’s times, Pithi ceremony is celebrated both bride and bride groom home separately in Hindu wedding. Pithi ceremony is one of the major auspicious occasions of Hindu marriage.

What is the Jaan Prastha?

Also known as baraat refers to the arrival of the groom - surrounded by family and friends - prior to the wedding ceremony. The groom often arrives with musical band and follows a procession of guests into the wedding venue. Once the groom arrives at the front of the venue, he is greeted by the bride's family. All guests are encouraged to attend the baraat which will begin from VITS Kamats Resort, Silvassa.

Will the wedding take place indoors or outdoors?

All the evens including Garba and wedding ceremony, cocktail hour will take place indoors. The Jaan Prasthan will take place outdoors.

Will there be vegetarian or Non vegetarian dining options at the events?

We will have a variety of Vegetarian food throughout the events and wedding to accommodate a range of dietary preferences, but if you have any strict dietary restrictions please let us know!
